Healthy Eating Habits


  • In order to control your diabetes, it is a good idea to maintain control of your diet
  • You can improve your diet by limiting your carbohydrate and sugar intakes
  • One way to limit your sugar intake is to not have as many sugary foods and drinks
  • When eating carbs, one should try to eat complex carbs, like vegetables and whole grains
  • Snack suggestions include: low-fat string cheese, boiled eggs, cucumbers, celery, sugar free gelatin, or a small serving of nuts
  • Here are some general percentage guidelines for calorie intake for your diet:
    • Proteins: 10-20%
    • Healthy fats: 25-30%
    • Carbohydrates: 50-60%
