How to Exercise with Diabetes


How to Safely Exercise with Type 1
  • Start your workout with your blood sugar in a safe range as discussed with your doctor
  • It is recommended to be careful the first time you exercise and make sure to have a caregiver with you
  • Eat extra carbohydrates if your insulin drops too low
  • Keep hydrated!!
  • Sports and exercises like soccer, basketball, swimming, walking, and biking are all great ways to stay active while living with Type 1 Diabetes
Timing your Workout
  • If you have a set workout time, prepare for it by lowering your insulin before it's time to workout
  • Exercising later in the day has a higher risk of having hypoglycemia overnight, where your blood sugar drops too low. To reduce this risk you can lower your insulin before the workout
